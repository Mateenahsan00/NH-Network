🌐 General Questions

What is NH Network? +
NH Network is a comprehensive web platform designed to combine structured cryptocurrency education with advanced trading tools. We help users transform from beginners into confident investors through real-time market data and professional learning paths.
Is NH Network free to join? +
Yes, creating an account on NH Network is completely free. You can sign up and explore the platform immediately. Certain features like advanced investment tools or specific certificate courses may require verification or specific account types.
How do I get started? +
Simply click the "Get Started" button on the home page, create your account, and choose your path. You can start as a Learner to build your knowledge or apply to become an Investor to start managing your portfolio.
Is NH Network available on mobile? +
NH Network is fully responsive and optimized for all devices, including smartphones and tablets. You can access your learning dashboard and investment portfolio anytime, anywhere, directly from your mobile browser.

👤 Account & Login

What are the password requirements? +
To ensure your account security, passwords must be at least 8 characters long and include at least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, one number, and one special character (e.g., @, #, $, %).
How can I reset my password? +
If you forget your password, click the "Forgot Password" link on the login form. Enter your registered email to receive a 6-digit OTP. Once verified, you can securely set a new password for your account.
Why haven't I received my OTP email? +
Please check your spam or junk folder. OTP emails usually arrive within 1-2 minutes. If you still don't see it, ensure the email address you entered is correct and click "Resend OTP" after the timer expires.
Is my personal information secure? +
Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and secure hashing (bcrypt) to protect your passwords and personal data. Your verification documents are stored securely and are only accessible for verification purposes.

📚 Learning Dashboard

How do I become a learner? +
Go to your dashboard and select "Become a Learner". Fill out the request form with your details and preferred course. Once submitted, our administrators will review and approve your access to the learning content.
How do I access video courses? +
Once your learner request is approved, you can access all video courses through the "Learn" tab in your learning dashboard. Each course is broken down into easy-to-watch lessons that you can follow sequentially.
What is the structure of the beginner course? +
Our beginner courses start with fundamental concepts like "What is Blockchain?" and "How Crypto Works." We then move to practical steps like setting up wallets and understanding market cycles to ensure a smooth learning curve.
How are the courses structured? +
Courses are organized into structured lessons with video content, detailed explanations, and progress tracking. You can learn at your own pace and see your completion percentage for each course in your dashboard.
What are the points in my dashboard? +
Points represent your engagement and achievements on the platform. You earn points by completing lessons, passing quizzes, and maintaining consistent learning habits. These points reflect your growing expertise in the crypto market.
Do I need prior experience to start learning? +
No prior experience is required! We offer beginner-friendly paths that cover the basics of blockchain and cryptocurrency, helping you build a solid foundation before moving on to more advanced investment strategies.
How does learning help before investing? +
Education is the best risk management tool. By understanding market mechanics, technical analysis, and project fundamentals before investing real capital, you can make more rational decisions and avoid common beginner pitfalls.

💰 Investment System

How do I become an investor? +
To start investing, you must complete the Investor Verification form. This requires basic personal information, a government-issued ID (CNIC), and a short verification video to ensure the security of our investment community.
What are the available investment plans? +
We offer flexible investment options where you can allocate your capital into various top-tier cryptocurrencies. Whether you are looking for long-term growth or short-term gains, our platform provides the tools to build a custom portfolio.
Where does the market data come from? +
We provide real-time market data through institutional-grade APIs (CoinGecko). This includes live prices, 24-hour price changes, and market capitalization for hundreds of cryptocurrencies to help you make informed decisions.
What are live coin updates? +
Live coin updates are real-time price feeds that refresh automatically. This ensures that the portfolio value and market prices you see are always current, allowing you to react quickly to market movements.
How do I buy or sell coins? +
Once your investor request is approved, you can use the funds in your wallet to purchase coins. The platform calculates the number of units based on live market prices. You can track your positions and sell them anytime to realize profits.

💸 Profits & Withdrawals

How do I track my investment profits? +
Your Investor Dashboard features a real-time Profit/Loss (PnL) tracker. It automatically compares your purchase price with current market values, displaying your net gain or loss both in currency and percentage.
How do I withdraw my funds? +
Navigate to the "Wallet" tab in your dashboard and select "Withdraw". Enter the amount you wish to withdraw and your preferred payment method. Withdrawals are processed securely after you confirm with your payment password.
Is there a fee for withdrawals? +
Yes, NH Network applies a standard 1% platform fee (tax) on all withdrawals. This small fee helps us maintain the infrastructure, live data feeds, and high-quality learning content for all our users.
How long do withdrawals take? +
Withdrawal requests are typically processed within 24-48 business hours. You can monitor the status of your withdrawal in the "Transaction History" section of your wallet dashboard.
Will I receive email confirmations for investments? +
Yes, for security and record-keeping, NH Network sends automated email confirmations for every major transaction, including deposits, withdrawals, and successful investment purchases.

🛡️ Security & Safety

How are my funds protected? +
We prioritize security through multi-layer protection, including secure database encryption, individual user wallets, and a secondary payment password required for all financial transactions on the platform.
What is the KYC process? +
Know Your Customer (KYC) is a standard verification process. We require ID documents and video verification to prevent fraud and ensure that all investors on our platform are genuine and verified individuals.
How do I resolve account access issues? +
If you are unable to access your account, first try resetting your password using the "Forgot Password" feature. If you still have trouble, contact our support team at devolper.expert@gmail.com for further assistance.
